Lasagna Recipe

Dish description

A traditional dish from the italian cousine which contain a creamy bachamel sauce and bolognese sauce

Ingredients

For the sauce

  • 500 grs of meat
  • 400 ml tomato sauce
  • Coriander
  • 5 tomatos
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 50ml of red wine
  • 1 spn of olive oil
  • Salt

For the Bachamel

  • 1 liter of milk
  • 200 grs of butter
  • 5 grs of black pepper
  • 2 Spoons of flour

For the Dough

  • 4 eggs
  • 400 grs of flour
  • salt
  • 1 spoon of Olive oil

Step by Step Preparation

  1. Preparing the dough

    2. For this step you need to pour the floor, the eggs and the oil into a
    bowl an merge them until unify and then extend the dough until thin

  2. Preparing the sauce

    3. Boil the tomatos until the shell get of easily
    On a different pan stirfrie the meat, onion and carrot together,
    salt them and add some spices. Once the tomatos are done, put them
    into the pan with the meat, add the tomato sauce, the milk, the wine
    and stir until merge everything up. Then leave it cooking and covered
    for like 1 - 2 hours and at the end add the coriander and a spoon of olive oil.

  3. The bechamel..

    4. To make the bechamel, the most importan ingredient is patience! Pour the milk, the butter and the flour into a cooking pot and stir for 30 minutes non-stop until it gets thicker!

  4. Is time to Layer up!

    5. Once we have everything done is time to build it up, start by adding
    some bechamel on the bottom, then add a layer of dough,
    in top of that add a layer of bolognese sauce mixed with a bit of bechamel
    and then repeat until the top, once the lasagna is done add some cheese on the top.

  5. To the heaven!... I mean the oven

    6. Now is time to put into the oven and wait 40min approximately

  6. Voila!

    7. And out delicious lasagna is done and ready to be eaten.. Yummy!
