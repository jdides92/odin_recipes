Magic Milkshake

Dish description

A magic milkshake potion to lift up even dead people!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 30 gr of protein powder unflavoured
  • 1 Mango
  • A cup of diced pineapple
  • 300 gr of milk
  • A cup of ice
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon powder

Step by Step Preparation

  1. Blending

    2. Take everything an put the ingredients into the blender and leave it for 5 to 10 minutes

  2. Serve it

    3. Serve it into a glass

  3. The final touch

    4. Add some magic cinnamon powder to enhace the flavour

  4. Voila!

    5. And voila is done and ready to be drunk.. Yummy!
