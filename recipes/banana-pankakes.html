Banana Pancakes

Dish description

Ingredients

A delicious recipe for a sweet breakfast

  • 100 grs of flour (replaceable with protein powder)
  • 100 ml of milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 egg
  • A bit of cinnamon
  • A bit of vanilla
  • Syrup for topping
  • A spoon of oil

Step by Step Preparation

  1. Blending

    2. Pour everything into a blender and lets go blend!

  2. Heat it up!

    3. Heat the pan up an add a drop of oil

  3. Pour the Mix

    4. Pour the mix into the pan and wait 2 minutes or until toasted to flip it

    Do this until use the bowl of mix

  4. And Voila!

    5. You have a tons of delicious banana pancakes ready to be eaten.. Yummy!
